Privacy

Categordical does not require an account. Your game progress is stored only on your device. When you submit a word to check if it is valid English, that word is sent over an encrypted connection to a third-party dictionary service (dictionaryapi.dev) to verify it. That service's own privacy policy applies to requests they receive. We do not sell your data. We do not receive the text for results on our servers if you choose to share or copy. We may update this policy. For questions, use the support email above. Operated by Zachary Burgess in the United States.
